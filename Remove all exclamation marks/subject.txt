Remove all exclamation marks from the end of sentence:


Description:

Remove all exclamation marks from the end of sentence.
Examples

"Hi!"     ---> "Hi"
"Hi!!!"   ---> "Hi"
"!Hi"     ---> "!Hi"
"!Hi!"    ---> "!Hi"
"Hi! Hi!" ---> "Hi! Hi"
"Hi"      ---> "Hi"


the function should be prototyped like this : 
char *remove_marks(const char *str_in, char *str_out);
