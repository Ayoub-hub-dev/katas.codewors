

Replace all vowel to exclamation mark in the sentence. "aeiouAEIOU" is vowel.


Examples:
|-------------------------------------|
| replace("Hi!") === "H!!"            |
| replace("!Hi! Hi!") === "!H!! H!!"  |
| replace("aeiou") === "!!!!!"        |
| replace("ABCDE") === "!BCD!"        |
|-------------------------------------|

The function should prototype like this :
char *replace(const char *s);
